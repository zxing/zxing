What's new in this version

New in version 3.0:

  • Added support for high resolution and high DPI screens.
  • Made the scanning rectangle wider for better performance on 1D barcodes.
  • Wrote a new history feature (press Menu from the scanning screen).
  • Added support for sharing contacts via ACTION_SEND.
  • Better performance and stability on Donut (Android 1.6) and later.
